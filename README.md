# GeoTrivia 2026 — References
 
A reference page for the GeoTrivia presented at the **UMaine Geotechnical Alumni & Friends Social**, held on May 29, 2026 in Portland, Maine.
 
The trivia was organized by the **UMaine Geotechnical Student Organization** as part of an evening of networking, faculty remarks, and friendly competition among geotechnical alumni and friends.
 
## About the Trivia
 
20 questions covering:
- History and famous figures in geotechnical engineering
- Real world projects and famous failures
- Maine-specific geotechnical challenges
- Ground improvement techniques
- Future of geotechnical engineering
